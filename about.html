Neeraj Bansal

SFU Mechatronic Student, PCB Designer, Guitar, Foosball, Basketball, Philosophy.

I am a fourth year Mechatronic Systems Engineering Student studying at Simon Fraser University in Canada. My interests are PCB designing, Embedded developer, robotics, medical devices, Networking and more. During my university, I joined 2 clubs Team Phantom and SFU Rocketry. Thanks to these teams, I learned a lot about different sub-systems of each project so check them out. Team Phantom is an electric formula one race car team who is planning to participate in FSAE Competition.

Currently I'm working on Networking Systems and IOT devices. In Team Phantom, I worked on designing the Power Distribution Board (Github Link) that takes 12V as an input and have multiple 12V and 5V as an output. Additionally, it has a protection circuit to protect the circuitry of the Low Voltage System of the car.

My work has been in the medical Equipment industry. Majority of my work has been related to designing PCB. Some of my other skills are Firmware development, User Interface Development, CNC Coder and machinist. I've worked at KAMCRAFT Kitchen Cabinets as a CNC Code designer.

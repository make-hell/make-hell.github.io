Team Phantom is a multidisciplinary engineering team from Simon Fraser University focused on designing and building a race-worthy electric vehicle for the Formula SAE Electric competition.

Electric Vehicle PCB Design Power Electronics Embedded Systems

Project Overview

Team Phantom worked towards developing an electric race car while promoting sustainable transportation technologies. The team progressed from converting a gasoline go-kart into an electric platform towards designing a competition-ready vehicle.

My role was part of the Electrical Team, where I focused on designing the Power Distribution Board responsible for managing the vehicle's Low Voltage System.
